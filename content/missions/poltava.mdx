Light of Hope charitable organization is an established domestic violence shelter, since the war began they have evacuated women and children from active combat zones and either housed them in 4 shelters located in Poltava or evacuated those who wish to Poland.  They also provide humanitarian aid to residents of the surrounding regions (Luhansk, Kharkiv, Sumy and Chernihiv) who are unable to evacuate.  They requested medicine, food, sanitary and hygiene supplies.  The medicine remains to be delivered.
